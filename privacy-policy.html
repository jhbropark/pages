Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

bbbb.beauty Social Publisher is an internal publishing tool used by the account owner to prepare and publish professional content to authorized social media profiles.

Information used

The tool uses OAuth authorization data supplied by LinkedIn, including an access token and the authenticated member identifier, only to identify the authorized account and publish content approved by that account owner.

How information is handled

OAuth credentials are stored as encrypted GitHub Actions secrets. They are not included in source code, public content, or application logs. The tool does not sell personal information or share it for advertising.

Retention and control

Authorization remains active until the token expires or the account owner revokes access through LinkedIn. Generated publishing records are retained only to operate and audit the content workflow.

Third-party services

Publishing is subject to the LinkedIn Privacy Policy and GitHub's applicable privacy and security terms.

Contact

Questions about this tool may be directed to bbbb.beauty through the LinkedIn profile that authorized the application.
